Quick Facts 🫠


  • 🤏 Height: 33 meters (108 feet).
  • 🏗️ Construction: Completed in 282 BC.
  • 👷‍♂️ Builder: Sculptor Chares of Lindos.
  • 🧱 Materials: Bronze and iron.
  • 🤔 Status: Destroyed by earthquake, 226 BC.
