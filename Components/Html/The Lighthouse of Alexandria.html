Quick Facts 🫠


  • 🤏 Height: Estimated 100 meters (330 feet).
  • 🏗️ Construction: Built circa 280 BCE.
  • 👷‍♂️ Architect: Sostratus of Cnidus.
  • 🧱 Materials: Light stone blocks.
  • 🤔 Status: Destroyed, 956–1323 CE.
